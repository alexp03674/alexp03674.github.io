    East Asian Eats

    Blacksburg is home to many East Asian cuisines. This section will highlight Japanese and Chinese restaurants.

    Kuma Moon

    Kuma Moon is a japanese restaurant with an incredibly diverse menu. Customers can purchase anything from sapporo ramen to matcha creme cakes.

    Menu Overview

    A key part of Kuma Moon is their abundance of ramen. They have pork, miso, spicy, and vegetarian ramen. As long as you like ramen, there is sure to be an option for you.

     Inferno ramen

    If you aren't in the mood for a brothy bowl, they even have "brothless ramen". Other non-ramen options consist of (but not limited to) Donburi, Udon, and Katsu Curry.

    This does not encompass everything yummy at Kuma Moon, but their menu will show you further options.

    Reviews and Pricing

    4.5 star rating | $10-20

    Many customers' reviews describe Kuma Moon as "cute ambience", "robust menu", and "delicious". Most enjoy the food, with the only negative reviews regarding wait time.

    Location and Atmosphere

    Kuma Moon is located on 725 University City Blvd, near the ABC store and CVS.

    ⚠️The parking lot is relatively small, with many spots saying "Truist Bank only".

    But, parking there after the bank's closed is never an issue. If the parking lot is really full, you can park at the nearby Panera and walk over.

    Kuma has a small, cozy atmosphere, typical of small japanese ramen shops. The kitchen is behind the bar, so not only can you see the food being made, but it can get a bit warm too!

    There is bar seating along with around 5 tables, along with some outdoor seating, but workers will push tables together for a big party.

    Personal Recommendation

    The inferno sapporo ramen is an amazing choice if you are a lover of spicy broth!

    Green's Grill and Sushi Bar

    Greens is a Lebanese owned restaurant with a diverse menu, predominantly of sushi.

    Menu Overview

    This diverse menu spans from mediterranean dishes to burgers to fish and chips, but we will focus on their east asian aspects of the menu.

    East asian appetizers consist of dumplings, shrimp, and gyoza. They have several different "asian bowls" ranging from Mongolian to Korean Bbq.

    Their sushi menu is quite expansive with specialty rolls, handmade rolls, and artisan rolls, and sashimi. Sushi burritos, bento boxes, and poke bowls are another interesting aspect of the menu that differentiates Green's from other sushi restaurants. Overall, Green's is a great restaurant for friends and families with diverse tastes.

    Reviews and Pricing

    3.6 star rating | $10-20

    The reviews of Green's tend to speak highly of the sushi. Some saying "beyond delicious" and "big menu, big portions". The low reviews mostly cite the service as being average or below.

    Many say the price is justifiable for the large portions.

    Location and Atmosphere

    109 College Ave It is located in the heart of downtown Blacksburg, so street parking is required.

    The restaurant is a decent size with lots of tables and some outside seating. Usually incredibly busy, so it can feel crowded. The decor is nice along with evident cleanliness.

    Personal Recommendations

    Even though the sushi is wonderful, my personal favorite is the fish and chips. This fish has a slight asian twist being fried in panko bread crumbs and served with asian side sauces. The fries are the perfect texture for fish and chips!

    Junzi Kitchen

    Junzi Kitchen is a Chinese restaurant with various kinds of fresh, ingredient forward bowls. This is a fast-casual Chinese restaurant that focuses on reimagined Northern Chinese comfort food.

    Menu Overview

    The menu centers around build-your-own grain bowls, noodle bowls, and salads, often featuring ingredients like braised pork, cumin lamb, tofu, seasonal vegetables, and house-made sauces. Signature items include cold noodle bowls and their popular “Beijing-style” dishes.

    Reviews and Pricing

    4.6 star rating | $10-20

    Many reviews emphasize the healthiness and variety of textures present in Junzi bowls.

    Location and Atmosphere

    220 Gilbert St Ste 115 Blacksburg

    The atmosphere is clean, modern, and minimal, with a focus on quick service and a relaxed, student-friendly environment. Customers can expect efficient service.

    Personal Recommendation

    I’d recommend trying a cumin lamb noodle bowl with hand-pulled noodles and chili oil. Tastes are rich, flavorful, and a great blend of spices that define Northern Chinese cuisine.
