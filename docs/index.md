# Let the Sims Begin

Welcome to _The_ [_Sims 4_](https://www.ea.com/en/games/the-sims/the-sims-4)_!_ _The Sims 4_ is a life simulation game where players create characters (sims) and control their day-to-day lives. Gameplay is open-ended with a variety of ways to play, including designing homes, fostering relationships, and choosing careers. There's no wrong way to play -- **just have fun!**
