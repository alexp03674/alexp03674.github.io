# Middle Eastern and Mediterranean Food
Blacksburg has multiple Middle Eastern or Mediterranean restaurants such as Mezeh, Kabob and More, and Souvlaki, but the best two restaurants of these cuisines combine them both: The Cellar and Italianos.

## The Cellar 
![Front view of The Cellar](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/p/AF1QipOUVeLcTeu4wWC2Rxd580d4rmz57SbK82vkKFYU=s1360-w1360-h1020-rw)
The Cellar is a casual Greek-American restaurant located in the heart of downtown Blacksburg, featuring two distinct seating areas - upstairs and downstairs.  

### Menu Overview
![Gryo from The Cellar](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/gps-cs-s/AHVAwephL9gE5e_ZxPgoxydWZ_FQ8nnuUkn1RlNJrdGDaMFr8lY4HJxNEuYdezfZW9Pypvpih4kjj7HGdzCuu34mAL34oBOPwWLb9L_9R6J0yMMQTi3N6GHM5zhAe9Ew9dPcAzNsrI6GEXRR1yg=s1360-w1360-h1020-rw)
The [menu](https://the-cellar.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/The-Cellar-Menu.pdf) at The Cellar offers traditional Greek-American cuisine alongside more traditional American bar food. 
Options include classic Greek appetizers such as spanakopita, souvlaki, gyro sandwiches, Italian style pastas, pizza, and American dishes like burgers and chicken tenders. 
>All food choices are the same upstairs and downstairs.

The Cellar is also known for their extensive drink menu, offering different menus for beer depending on if you are seated [upstairs](https://the-cellar.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/02/Beer-List-Upstairs.pdf) or [downstairs](https://the-cellar.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/02/Beer-List-Downstairs.pdf), with a universal [cocktail](https://the-cellar.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/01/Cocktails.pdf) and [shooter](https://the-cellar.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/01/Shooters.pdf) menu.

### Reviews and Pricing
4.4 star rating | $10-20

Customer reviews highlight the high quality food, great atmosphere, and friendly staff. 

### Location and Atmosphere
The Cellar offers a very casual atmosphere regardless if you sit upstairs or downstairs. However, the upstairs is best for families or for those searching for a more standard dining experience, with the downstairs featuring dark bar lighting best for college students.

Located at [302 N Main St. Blacksburg,](https://www.google.com/maps?rlz=1C1VDKB_enUS1015US1015&um=1&ie=UTF-8&fb=1&gl=us&sa=X&geocode=Kdf1RI90lU2IMbTmMu63YaON&daddr=302+N+Main+St,+Blacksburg,+VA+24060) The Cellar has a parking lot directly behind it that requires payment during weekdays and is free at night and during the weekend. 

### Recommendations 
The Cellar’s menu is truly fantastic, with many great choices, but the pizzas are a personal favorite, as you can customize them, choosing up to three toppings for no additional charge. 

## Italianos 
![The Storefront of Italianos](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/gps-cs-s/AHVAweodPMIzfJEElUDKtme6YB2nNDCygYdjhr7UjhUKj7fgw5_sw5pj62JvaS9jv5EP_82NNT3Db5v3HeuYAEiZPqua4BCQYsTMooFd2wJ6hx6sr6vlKelzd8qqWczq3FuHfxIkolDswA=w289-h312-n-k-no)
Italianos is a casual Italian and Middle Eastern restaurant that offers quick and easy Italian and Halal food with great portions.  

### Menu Overview
The [menu](https://www.orderitalianospizzamenu.com/) at Italianos offers a wide range of Mediterranean and halal dishes. They specialize in making standard, specialty, and thick crust pizzas, salads, calzones, strombolis, burgers, sandwiches, pasta, and halal gyros and rice plates. 

Standard pizzeria sides are available such as breadsticks and mozzarella sticks; and there is a small dessert section featuring cheesecake, cannolis, and baklava. 

### Reviews and Pricing
4.1 star rating | $10-20

Reviews highlight the great affordability and high portions of the delicious pizzas, pastas, and halal dishes. 

### Location and Atmosphere
The interior of the restaurant is incredibly small, offering only a few areas to sit and eat, which makes Italianos a great carryout and delivery restaurant - especially considering the quick service time. 

The restaurant is located at [610 N Main St. Blacksburg,](https://www.google.com/maps?rlz=1C1VDKB_enUS1015US1015&gs_lcrp=EgZjaHJvbWUqDggAEEUYJxg7GIAEGIoFMg4IABBFGCcYOxiABBiKBTIMCAEQIxgnGIAEGIoFMgoIAhAuGLEDGIAEMg0IAxAAGJIDGIAEGIoFMg0IBBAAGJIDGIAEGIoFMg0IBRAAGIMBGLEDGIAEMg0IBhAuGK8BGMcBGIAEMg0IBxAuGK8BGMcBGIAEMgoICBAAGMkDGIAEMgoICRAuGLEDGIAE0gEIMTAzN2owajGoAgCwAgA&um=1&ie=UTF-8&fb=1&gl=us&sa=X&geocode=Kd-cLdVzlU2IMdaqmHHJQ_D9&daddr=610+N+Main+St+Ste+B,+Blacksburg,+VA+24060) right next to the downtown Waffle House. Parking is available directly in front of the restaurant. 
### Recommendations
![Halal gyro from Italianos](https://slice-menu-assets-prod.imgix.net/16168/1609174772_4079957032?fit=crop&w=2000&h=1250)
The halal gyro with lamb and chicken is one of the most popular dishes - and my personal recommendation. The meat is always cooked well and paired with delicious sauces and a side of fries.
