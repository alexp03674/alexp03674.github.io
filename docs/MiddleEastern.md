# Middle Eastern and Mediterranean Eats
## Italianos 
While Italianos lives up to its name with an extensive menu of italian food, it also includes a great selection of Halal food. 

### Atmosphere 
The inside of the restraunt is small, with only a few tables. Most people order delivery or do take-out. The staff working is usually very friendly, helpful, and grateful towards the customers. 

### Menu Overview 
Italianos offers pizzas, thick crust sicilian pizza, appetizers, salads, calzones, strambolis, halal sandwhiches, burgers, halal gyros and rice plates, pasta, desserts. 

### Price 
The food at Italianos is reasonable priced and the portions are generally large. Most larger meal items, such as the rice plates, gryros, and calzones have large portions that can last up to two meals. 

### Location 
Italianos is located on North Main Street at 610 N Main St Ste B, Blacksburg, VA 24060 near the edge of downtown Blacksburg. There is parking on site, directly in front of the building. The parking lot also has ample space for cars to quickly park in front of the restaurant for online orders. 

### Recommendations
Most people get the lamb or mixed lamb and chicken over rice and gyros. Despite the name Italianos, they are best known around Blacksburg for these great halal options.
