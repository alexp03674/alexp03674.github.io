# South Asian Cuisine

Blacksburg has several South and Southeast Asian restaurants to dine at. From India, to Nepal, to Thailand, there's an option for everyone!

## Namaste Kitchen

![Photo of Namaste Kitchen's exterior](https://bloximages.newyork1.vip.townnews.com/collegiatetimes.com/content/tncms/assets/v3/editorial/c/7f/c7f7b173-34ca-420c-bb38-edd96e175f8c/68e2b157d005e.image.jpg)
[Namaste's](https://namastekitchen.net/) cuisine is mixed: They offer both Indian and Nepali dishes.

### Menu Overview

![Biryani](https://tb-static.uber.com/prod/image-proc/processed_images/eb06b07815afbb2cfa34aa019907401f/c67fc65e9b4e16a553eb7574fba090f1.jpeg)
Namaste serves a variety of entrees, including tandoori, biryani, chow mein, fried rice, and goat, lamb, and chicken-based meals. You can find their full menu by clicking the top left button on their website. 

If you don't eat meat, you're in luck. Namaste also offers many options for vegetarians, including dedicated lists of vegetarian appetizers, entrees, and momos, which are bite-sized dumplings that originate in Tibet. 

### Reviews and Pricing

Namaste has a Google rating of 4.6 stars. | $20-30

>⚠️A filling meal will likely be on the higher end.

### Location and Atmosphere

This restaurant is located downtown at [239 North Main](https://share.google/EEjd1lGNddQVQ3zOU) Street. You'll find it on the corner just before the turn onto College Avenue by the Squire's parking lot. 

Inside, the restaurant is cozy and well decorated. It can get a bit loud and crowded when full, but the staff has been described as kind and attentive to their customers. 

>⚠️Service can get slow during peak hours. Make sure to check the restaurant's activity on Google.

### Personal Recommendation

A bit of a predictable answer, but the Chicken Tikka Masala here is really good.

## Hamro Kitchen

![Hamro's logo](https://hamrokitchen.net/logo.png)
Another kitchen for your consideration! [Hamro](hamrokitchen.net) is a Nepali restaurant.

### Menu Overview

![Spread of dishes including curry, rice, and momos](https://tb-static.uber.com/prod/image-proc/processed_images/24f3b3f1204a21810765c0715831910d/3ac2b39ad528f8c8c5dc77c59abb683d.jpeg)
Hamro offers a wide range of Nepali cuisine. You can find the full menu [here](https://hamrokitchen.net/menu). They have biryani, momo, tandoori, and dedicated vegetarian options. Meat entrees come in chicken, lamb, mutton, and seafood, served with basmati rice.

### Reviews and Pricing

Hamro has a Google rating of 4.6 stars. | $10-20

### Location and Atmosphere

You can find Hamro in the southern part of downtown Blacksburg at [403 Draper Road NW](https://share.google/JnviXvkpPdNwpkN6J). It's just off South Main Street by the Blacksburg Public Library. 

### Personal Recommendation

Hamro’s paneer dishes are really good. I’d recommend getting either the mutter paneer or the paneer tikka masala.

## Cafe Mekong

![Photo of Cafe Mekong's exterior](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/gps-cs-s/AHVAwerRbBKnULpZE4CpZhEigbNtQNOQ94u_sHQQP-itrQNpvm_NZYu-grQg5JG2C4zUiilXhk8dEBCEuTntfB6E6asFQ3i-D7LuYlWPZrQ-OuPQ_cxjxhEW1-9fpVSZchmv_GkkIblj=s1360-w1360-h1020-rw)
[Cafe Mekong](https://www.cafe-mekong.com/) is a Thai and Vietnamese restaurant. 

### Menu Overview

![Pho and fried rice](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/gps-cs-s/AHVAweqJkajjLVOwRn4aaycyjwUhIPf_VfYnDYqeZ44HFfPPxKJa-6hMdloNQ8529XP7n-1aWJj0XQdMx4JVlhfQt1L9JE09GkeIe-z83T9gZVP3lX61wt-a-jcOv8k2AqMPTG7raSwp=s1360-w1360-h1020-rw)
Cafe Mekong offers Thai curries, noodles like pad thai and pad siew, fried rice entrees, and several Vietnamese dishes including pho. They also have a few options from other Asian countries like Korea, China, and Singapore. You’ll find them in their “Special Entrees” section. You can find their full menu [here]( https://www.cafe-mekong.com/MekongImages/CurrMenu.pdf).

At the end of the menu, you can find vegan versions of some of their most popular noodle and rice dishes.

### Reviews and Pricing

This cafe has a Google rating of 4.5 stars. | $10-20

### Location and Atmosphere

Cafe Mekong is located on [1405 South Main Street](https://share.google/879zNaMvWDRqVdNAr), across the street from Kroger. You'll find it right next to Oasis. 

This restaurant is small, but it has plenty of seating and a cozy feel. Since it's located in a plaza with a grocery store, there's also plenty of parking right out front. Service tends to be pretty quick and many reviewers have mentioned that the staff there is helpful, kind, and provides good customer service.

### Personal Recommendation

I love the panang curry from here. I usually get mine with tofu, but you can choose any of their other protein options.
