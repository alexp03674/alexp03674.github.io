About

My name is Alex Panov and I am a contemporary acrylic painter and visualzie artist. This website serves as my virtual gallery to showcase my paintings and drawings and the collections that they reside in. My collections are similar to albums, where I compile or create paintings to fit into an overarching experience, whether narratively or visually. With that said, online isn't the best way to experience any art, so please me at sampleemail@address.com if interested in purchasing anything.
